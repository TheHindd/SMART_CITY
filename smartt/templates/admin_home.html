About Cyprus


Cyprus is an island country in the Eastern Mediterranean and the third largest and third most populous island in the Mediterranean, located south of Turkey, west of Syria and Lebanon, northwest of Israel, north of Egypt, and southeast of Greece.

It is known for its rich history, stunning beaches, and vibrant culture. The capital city is Nicosia.

Explore the beauty of Cyprus with its ancient ruins, picturesque villages, and delicious cuisine.
